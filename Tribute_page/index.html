Zinadine Zidane

" A French Former Professional Football Player who played as an Attacking Midfielder and Current Manager of Real Madrid "

Zidane as Manager of Real Madrid

Here's a time line of Zinadine Zidane's life:

  • 1972 - Born in Marseile, France
  • 1989 - Zidane made his professional debut with Cannes
  • 1991 - Scored his first goal for the club against nantes
  • 1992 - Transferred to Girondins de Bordeaux
  • 1994 - Earned his first cap for France as subtitute in friendly match against Czech Republic
  • 1996 - Offered to join Europe's top clubs in the spring to UEFA Champions League winners, Juventus
  • 1997 - Won A Serie A title and receiver Serie A Foreign Footballer of the Year
  • 1998 - Won FIFA World Cup, received the Légion d'honneur, FIFA World Player of the Year and FIFA Ballon d'Or
  • 2000 - Won EURO Cup and named Player of the Tournament
  • 2001 - Received Serie A Foreign Footballer of the Year and Joined Real Madrid for a World record fee of 150 billion Italian lire or €77.5 million
  • 2002 - Won UEFA Champions League with Real Madrid
  • 2003 - Helped Real Madrid to win La-Liga and named the FIFA World Player of the Year for the third time
  • 2004 - received the best European footballer of the previous 50 years in UEFA's fiftieth-anniversary Golden Jubilee Poll.
  • 2006 - announced his plans to retire after the 2006 World Cup and carried France to The Final World Cup.
